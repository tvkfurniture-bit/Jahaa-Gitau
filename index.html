Frequently Asked Questions

Do you handle NEMA and County Council approvals?

Yes. We provide a full-service experience which includes managing all government permits, site inspections, and formal approvals to ensure your project is 100% compliant.

What is the advantage of a Designer-Contractor hybrid?

It prevents the "translation gap." Because we design what we build, your house is guaranteed to match the architectural blueprints without the structural errors common when hiring two separate firms.

Do you work on difficult soil types like Black Cotton?

Absolutely. With 14 years of experience in the Nairobi region, we specialize in advanced foundation engineering for all terrain types, ensuring lifelong structural integrity.

How do you manage the rising cost of materials?

We provide a detailed Bill of Quantities (BQ) and use our industrial supply network to lock in prices at the start of each construction phase, protecting you from sudden market fluctuations.

Do you handle projects outside of Nairobi?

Yes. While we are based in Nairobi, our team has the capacity to manage high-end residential and commercial builds across Kenya and the wider East African region.

Can I monitor the progress of my build remotely?

Yes. Every client is provided with a digital progress log. You can see project milestones, photos of the site, and budget updates live from your phone or computer.
